The Honor 90 Pro screen may become unresponsive due to various reasons. Here are some common situations and their possible causes:

- **During Use/Gaming:** The screen may become unresponsive due to overheating when using or gaming on a Honor 90 Pro phone. This can be caused by running too many apps simultaneously or playing games for an extended period, especially on models such as the Honor 90 Pro 9 Pro, which generates much heat during gaming.

- **No Response When Touching the Screen:**Sometimes, the Honor 90 Pro screen may not respond when touched due to a faulty touch screen or display. This can happen due to physical damage or a manufacturing defect. Some Honor 90 Pro Nord 2 users have reported this issue.

- **After Dropping the Phone:** If a Honor 90 Pro phone has been dropped, the screen may become unresponsive due to physical damage or a loose connection. This can happen to any model, including the Honor 90 Pro.

Overheating, physical damage, and manufacturing defects are some common causes of Honor 90 Pro screen unresponsiveness, which may occur during use/gaming, no response when touching the screen, or after dropping the phone.
